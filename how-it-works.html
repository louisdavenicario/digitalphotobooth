How It Works

Step 1: Start the Photobooth

Tap anywhere on the welcome screen to begin.

Step 2: Grant Camera Access

Your browser will ask for camera permissions. Allow access to proceed.

Step 3: Adjust Your Pose

Position yourself properly. The camera preview will guide you.

Step 4: Capture Photos

Tap the camera button once to take 4 photos automatically at 3 seconds intervals.

Step 5: Review Your Photostrip

After capturing, your images will be displayed in a photostrip. You can change the frame color to match your preference.

Step 6: Download Your Photostrip

Click the Download button to save your photostrip as a PNG image.

Step 7: Retake If Needed

If you're not happy with the results, tap Back to Camera to retake your photos.

Note:

Refreshing the page will automatically return you to the home screen.

About Digital Photobooth

The Digital Photobooth is a minimalistic web-based photo-taking experience, inspired by boothbybryant. Users can capture, preview, and download their photostrips instantly. All captured images remain private and are not stored or uploaded to any external server.

Privacy Policy

1. No Image Storage: All photos are processed directly on your browser and are not saved on any server.
2. No Third-Party Access: Your images are never shared or used for any purpose outside of your session.
3. Browser-Based: Once you leave the page, all captured images are gone unless downloaded manually.
4. User Control: You have full control over whether you save or discard your images.
